# Hello Writespace!

Gather all of your notes in one place, access them from anywhere.

  • Writespace speaks markdown and includes a load of handy shortcuts to make editing a snap

  • Notes are synced to a secure server so they’re always safely backed up

  • Writespace works offline. Notes are saved locally and synced when you’re back online
